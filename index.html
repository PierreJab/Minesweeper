Minesweeper Rules

  • The goal of Minesweeper is to discover all free boxes without triggering the explosion of a bomb
  • Click to discover a box
  • Right Click to place a flag
  • The numbers displayed on discovered boxes indicate how many mines are around : left, right, above, under, or even in diagonal. Then maximum is 8 bombs around
  • Thanks to those indications, you can deduce where are the bombs so you avoid them. If a box has number 1, and there is only one box that has not been discovered yet around, this box is hiding a bomb
  • The counter at the top right of the grid indicates how many bombs you still have to avoid
  • On the left of the grid is the timer, finish as soon as possible !
